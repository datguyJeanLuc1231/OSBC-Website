Meet Our Pastor

Rev. Camara Blue

And how shall they preach, except they be sent? as it is written, How beautiful are the feet of them that preach the gospel of peace, and bring glad tidings of good things!

~ Romans 10:15

Our First Family

Pastor Camara Blue is a native of Birmingham, Alabama. He holds a B.S. degree in Communication from Miles College and a Master's of Divinity degree from Morehouse School of Religion. In March of 2006, Pastor Blue preached his very first sermon. Since then, he has been preaching the gospel of Jesus Christ. Pastor Blue has a deep passion for helping the youth and the black community as a whole. He currently works at The Dannon Project teaching conflict resolution and restorative justice classes.

Pastor Blue is an active member of the Beta Mu Nu Chapter of Omega Psi Phi, Inc. Through the fraternity, he mentors youth through the Lifting as We Climb Foundation and Black Rhinos of Alabama.

Pastor Blue is married to Dr. LaTandra Blue. She has a B.S. in Biology and a Doctorate in Optometry both from the University of Alabama at Birmingham. She is an active member of the Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc. She also was recently sworn in as a Captain in the United States Army Reserve.

Pastor Blue is the father of four beautiful children: Dequawn(20), Ja'Marcus(17), McKenna(4), and Camara II(2)
